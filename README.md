🔹 Objective

“Amazon Sales Data Analysis: Insights into Customer Behavior & Revenue Drivers”

🔹 Tools Used
Python
Pandas
Matplotlib
Seaborn
🔹 Key Insights
Discounts influence sales
Ratings impact customer decisions
Few products drive most revenue

This analysis highlights that customer purchasing decisions are largely influenced by pricing and trust factors such as product ratings and reviews. It also shows that a significant portion of revenue comes from a small set of high-performing products. Additionally, discounts play an important role in driving sales. By refining pricing strategies and focusing on top-performing categories, the business can improve both revenue and overall profitability.
